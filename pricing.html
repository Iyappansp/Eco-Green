Frequently Asked Questions

How are minutes calculated? +

Minutes are calculated based on the actual duration of the audio file submitted. For example, a 15-minute consultation recording is billed as 15 minutes regardless of specialty.

Are there setup fees? +

No setup fees or monthly minimums. You only pay for transcription minutes when you use the service.

Can I adjust my plan anytime? +

Yes. You can switch between Standard, Urgent, and STAT plans for any job at any time without penalties.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes. We offer flexible payment options including monthly invoicing for qualified customers with established relationships.

What if my audio is poor quality? +

We still process poor-quality audio with our specialist team. Extreme cases may incur additional review charges which we'll discuss upfront.
