About EcoRefill

Building a zero-waste future, one refill at a time

Our Story

EcoRefill was founded in 2020 by a group of environmental enthusiasts who saw a problem: the world generates 381 million tons of plastic waste annually, yet simple solutions weren't accessible to everyday people.

We started with a simple mission: make sustainable living accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone. Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 members who have collectively saved 2.4 million kilograms of plastic from landfills.

Our platform empowers people to shop responsibly, track their environmental impact in real-time, and become part of a global movement toward zero-waste living.
