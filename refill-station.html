Refill Questions

What containers can I bring?

Any clean, food-grade containers like glass jars, stainless steel bottles, cloth bags, or plastic containers. We don't accept containers with odors or residue.

Do I need to register before refilling?

No, you can refill without membership. However, registered members get discounts and earn refill credits that can be redeemed.

What are the opening hours?

Monday-Sunday: 9:00 AM - 7:00 PM. We're closed on public holidays. Book a slot in advance for guaranteed service.

Can I order online and refill later?

Yes! Order online, choose a refill pickup slot, and collect your products at the scheduled time. We'll keep your order fresh for you.
